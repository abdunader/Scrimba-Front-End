Top three activities to do at Egypt

Visit the Muhammad Ali Mosque

The Muhammad Ali Mosque is built between 1830 and 1848, the Citadel of Saladin is the largest mosque constructed in the nineteenth century and the most visible mosque in Cairo

Take a tour in the Egyptian Museum

The Museum houses a large number of other artefacts discovered in Tutankhamen's tomb, including a decorated chest, ivory and gold bracelets and other jewellery and weapons.

Visit the Pyramids

The Pyramid remained the tallest man-made structure for almost four millennia, until the building of the spire to Lincoln Cathedral in 1300.
